CO2 Emission by vehicles in 2017 & 2018

Personal vehicles are a major source of global warming. Nearly one fifth of all US emissions, emitting approx 24 lbs of carbon dioxide and other global-warming gases for every gallon of gas.

Transporation accounts for largest contributor of US Greenhouse Gases emission i.e. 29%.

Adopting Fuel-efficient vehicles, Cleaner fuels, Electric cars and trucks etc. can help us control greenhouse effect.
